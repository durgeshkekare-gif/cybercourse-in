DPDP Act — how it's changing cybersecurity salaries in India

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 is creating a surge in demand for data security, compliance, and privacy-focused cybersecurity roles. Companies face fines up to ₹250 crore for non-compliance. This is driving salary increases of 20–35% for roles at the intersection of cybersecurity + compliance: Data Protection Officer (DPO), Chief Compliance Officer, and Cybersecurity Risk Manager. The IIM Indore AI & Cybersecurity programme is currently the only IIM-branded course covering DPDP Act in depth.

Cybersecurity career path — entry to CISO

Years 1–3: SOC Analyst / IT Security Analyst. CompTIA Security+ or CEH. ₹3.5–7L.
Years 4–7: Security Engineer / Pen Tester / Cloud Security. CEH + CISSP L1. ₹7–16L.
Years 8–12: Cybersecurity Manager / Architect. CISSP + IIM cert. ₹16–35L.
Years 13+: CISO / VP Cybersecurity. CISSP + executive management credential. ₹35–80L+.
