IIM Indore AI & Cybersecurity Programme 2026 — Complete Review

IIM Indore AI & Cybersecurity — what is it?

The Executive Programme in Artificial Intelligence & Cybersecurity for Organizations from IIM Indore is unique — the only IIM program that combines AI, ML, Big Data and Cybersecurity in a single 12-month certificate. IIM Indore is Triple-Crown accredited (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS) and NIRF #7 in Management. Fee approximately ₹3–4L + GST. Non-engineers are explicitly welcomed — no coding requirement.

Who is it for?

IT managers and project managers responsible for both digital security and AI adoption. Operations and compliance heads. Risk management professionals in BFSI, healthcare and manufacturing. Senior managers in digital transformation roles. Professionals targeting CISO, Chief AI Officer, or Digital Risk Head roles. Minimum 2 years work experience. Graduate in any discipline.

Curriculum

AI Track: AI fundamentals, ML for managers, Big Data analytics, Power BI, Tableau, GenAI concepts, blockchain, quantum computing primer. Cybersecurity Track: Threat landscape, risk management frameworks, incident response, SOC2, ISO 27001, GDPR, India's DPDP Act compliance, cloud security. Integration modules: managing AI systems as security assets and attack surfaces — the most relevant curriculum for 2026's CISO profile.

Is ₹3–4L worth it?

The AI + Cybersecurity combination is one of the highest-demand skill sets in India in 2026. The IIM Indore Triple-Crown credential plus this dual specialisation positions candidates strongly for CISO and Chief AI Officer roles that routinely pay ₹40–100L+. The break-even on ₹3–4L investment typically occurs within 12–18 months through role change or salary upgrade. See all cyber programs at cybercourse.in/compare.
