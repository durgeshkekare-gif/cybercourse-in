Network Security Courses India 2026 — Top Certifications & Career Guide

Network security career paths in India — two tracks

Before picking a certification, understand which track you're targeting. Technical track: ethical hacking, pen testing, red team, malware analysis, incident response — certifications: CEH → OSCP → GREM. Management track: SOC lead, security architect, CISO, risk and compliance — certifications: Security+ → CISSP → IIM/IIT management cert. Many professionals start technical and shift management as they gain experience.

Top cyber security bootcamps and free resources India 2026

Free resources to build foundational skills before spending on paid certifications: TryHackMe.com (gamified cybersecurity learning, ₹0–₹1,500/month), HackTheBox (advanced labs, ₹0–₹2,500/month), NPTEL Cybersecurity courses (free IIT-quality content), Cybrary (free tier). These are where most Indian ethical hackers build practical skills before sitting for CEH or OSCP.

DPDP Act compliance roles — the new security category 2026

India's DPDP Act 2023 has created significant demand for professionals who combine cybersecurity knowledge with regulatory compliance expertise. These roles pay a 25–40% premium over pure technical roles: Data Protection Officer (₹15–35L), Compliance & Privacy Manager (₹12–25L), Cybersecurity GRC Analyst (₹10–20L). For this track, the IIM Indore Executive Programme in AI & Cybersecurity is the strongest management credential covering DPDP Act compliance.
